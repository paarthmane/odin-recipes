Butter Chicken

Description

Indulge your taste buds in the rich and aromatic flavors of our Creamy Butter Chicken recipe. This beloved classic from the heart of Indian cuisine is a symphony of tender, marinated chicken pieces bathed in a luscious, velvety tomato-based sauce. Every bite is a harmonious blend of warm spices, creamy butter, and the essence of smoky tandoor-grilled chicken.

To prepare this delectable dish, succulent chicken pieces are first marinated in a tantalizing mixture of yogurt, ginger, garlic, and a medley of Indian spices. This marinade infuses the meat with a depth of flavor and ensures each bite is juicy and flavorful.

The pièce de résistance of this dish is the buttery tomato sauce. Fresh tomatoes are simmered to perfection, their natural sweetness enhanced by aromatic spices such as cumin, coriander, and garam masala. As the sauce bubbles away, it absorbs the richness of butter, cream, and a hint of honey, resulting in a luxurious, velvety texture that's both indulgent and comforting.

Ingredients

  • Chicken pieces (boneless or bone-in)
  • Yogurt
  • Ginger garlic paste
  • Garam masala
  • Cumin powder
  • Coriander powder
  • Turmeric powder
  • Red chili powder
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Cooking oil
  • Fresh tomatoes
  • Heavy cream
  • Honey
  • Fresh cilantro (coriander leaves) for garnish

Steps

  1. Marinate the Chicken:
    • In a bowl, combine yogurt, ginger garlic paste, garam masala, cumin powder, coriander powder, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt.
    • Add chicken pieces to the marinade, ensuring they are well-coated. Allow them to marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Prepare the Tomato Sauce:
    • Heat a combination of butter and cooking oil in a pan.
    • Add fresh tomatoes and cook until they turn soft and the oil begins to separate.
  3. Blend the Tomato Sauce:
    • Let the tomato mixture cool slightly, then blend it into a smooth sauce using a blender or immersion blender.
  4. Cook the Marinated Chicken:
    • In a separate pan, heat some more butter.
    • Add the marinated chicken pieces and cook until they are no longer pink.
  5. Simmer the Chicken in the Sauce:
    • Pour the tomato sauce over the cooked chicken.
    • Add heavy cream and honey to the pan.
    • Simmer the mixture until the chicken is fully cooked and the sauce thickens.
  6. Garnish and Serve:
    • Before serving, garnish the Butter Chicken with fresh cilantro (coriander leaves).
  7. Serve with Accompaniments:
    • Serve the Butter Chicken hot with steamed rice or warm naan bread.
